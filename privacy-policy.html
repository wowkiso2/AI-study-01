Welcome to LottoGen ("we", "us", or "our"). We operate the website located at this domain (the "Site"). We may collect information about you in various ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

  • Log Data: Like many websites, we automatically collect certain information when you visit the Site, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and the pages you visit. This information is used for analyzing trends, administering the site, and diagnosing technical problems.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Site and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you do not accept cookies, some portions of our Site may not function properly.
  • Contact Form Data: If you submit a business inquiry through our contact form (powered by Formspree), we collect your email address and the message you submit. This data is processed by Formspree, Inc. in accordance with their privacy policy.

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To operate and maintain our Site
