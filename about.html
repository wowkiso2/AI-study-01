About LottoGen

A free, fair, and unbiased Korean Lotto 6/45 number generator

Our Mission

LottoGen was created with a simple goal: to give every Lotto player access to a fast, reliable, and completely unbiased random number generator for Korean Lotto 6/45. We believe that choosing your numbers should be easy, free, and fun — without any gimmicks, misleading claims, or paid subscriptions.

We also believe in transparency. That's why we are upfront about how our generator works, what the real odds are, and what responsible lottery play looks like. We don't promise guaranteed wins or sell "hot number" systems — because mathematically, they don't work.

How the Generator Works

Our number generator uses JavaScript's built-in pseudorandom number generator (PRNG), which is based on the Xorshift128+ algorithm in modern browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. This algorithm produces statistically independent, uniformly distributed random numbers across the range 1 to 45.

Every time you press the "Generate Lucky Numbers" button, the following happens:

  1. Six unique random integers between 1 and 45 are independently generated.
  2. The numbers are sorted in ascending order for easy reading.
  3. The results are displayed with a brief animation.

No number is ever "more likely" than another. No past results are stored or factored in. Each generation is completely independent of any previous or future draw. This is the same mathematical fairness you can expect from any properly conducted physical lottery.

Our Commitment to Responsible Play

The lottery can be a fun and exciting form of entertainment. However, we take responsible gambling seriously. Our site includes information about realistic odds, prize structures, and links to official problem gambling support resources in Korea.

We strongly encourage all users to:

  • Set a strict personal budget for lottery spending and adhere to it.
  • Understand that the lottery is a game of chance, not a financial strategy.
  • Seek help immediately if gambling negatively affects their lives.

If you or someone you know needs support, please contact the Korea Problem Gambling Helpline at 1336 (available 24/7, free of charge).
